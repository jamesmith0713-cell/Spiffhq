How it works

Four steps. Every pay period. On repeat.

Once you set it up — which takes about two minutes — it runs itself. Same flow for every rep, every period, regardless of whether you're running daily bonuses, weekly spiffs, or a month-long contest.

1

Rep logs a sale

Picks their name, enters their count per spiff type, attaches a reference. 30 seconds, done.

2

Manager verifies

Runs your custom checklist against the real record. Initials it. Entry goes from pending to approved.

3

Board updates live

Everyone sees standings in real time. Tiered rates and bonus thresholds kick in automatically — no one recalculates anything.

4

Export and pay

One CSV. Approved entries only, broken down by rep and incentive type. Hand it to payroll and move on.

FAQ

Stuff people ask before they sign up.

Does my whole team need to create accounts?

No. Only you — the admin — create a login. Your team uses a single shared link. They pick their name from a dropdown and log sales instantly. No passwords for reps, no onboarding headache for you.

Can I run a daily contest and a monthly bonus program at the same time?

Yes. Each incentive type has its own reset period — daily, weekly, or monthly. The leaderboard shows separate tabs and tracks each program completely independently.

We don't call them spiffs — we call them bonuses or contests. Does that matter?

Not at all. SpiffHQ doesn't use your terminology in front of your team — you name every incentive type yourself. Whatever your team calls them, the system tracks them the same way.

Can I use this for a car dealership? A tax office? A pest control company?

Yes. The incentive types, verification steps, and reference options are all things you configure yourself. SpiffHQ doesn't assume your industry — it lets you define how your program works, in your language.

What happens if I want to change plans?

Upgrade or downgrade any time, no penalties. Your data stays with you regardless of which plan you're on. If you downgrade, you keep all your entries and settings.

How does the per-rep pricing work exactly?

Your base fee covers a set number of reps — 3 on Starter, 10 on Pro. Add more reps beyond that and you pay a small flat rate per additional rep per month. So a 6-rep team on Starter pays $19 + (3 × $3) = $28/month. Remove a rep and your bill adjusts the next month. No surprises.
