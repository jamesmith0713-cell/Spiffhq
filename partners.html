The problem

Running promotions is easy. Tracking them is where it breaks.

Every owner we talk to gives the same answer when we ask how they track their bonus programs: a spreadsheet, a whiteboard, or a group text. And every one of them has had the same argument.

📊
The spreadsheet is always wrong somewhere. Tiered rates, doubling thresholds, combined contests — someone recalculates it by hand every pay period. Someone gets a wrong number. Nobody wins that conversation.
🤝
You're paying on the honor system. If no one's checking entries against real records before payout, you're not running a program — you're running a trust exercise. When it goes wrong, it comes out of your pocket.
⏰
It takes time you don't have. Reconciling counts, fixing wrong payouts, answering "what do I owe everyone" — that's time you should be running your business, not your spreadsheet.
